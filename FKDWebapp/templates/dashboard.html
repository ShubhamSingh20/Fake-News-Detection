This application works with the help of deep learning and natural language processesing. A neural network model is pre trained on a dataset of 7000+ news articles which is labeled either as reliable or non-reliable (Note:It does not assert a 100% accuracy, only reliability). Now for every url entered by the user the web app will call a parser which will extract the article text and santitize it for proper input. Keywords are extracted from the article corpus and then a web scrapper will scrape the internet for simial news with similar tags, and extracts the text and feed it to a presisted neural network, which will give the bias from all the post. Collective bias of all the articles will give the final result whether the article at the given site is reliable or not.
